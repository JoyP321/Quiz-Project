Jazz Band Quiz Game!

This game will cover major, minor, major pentatonic, and minor pentatonic scales

Question 1) How do you convert a major scale to a minor scale?

You flat the third, sixth, and seventh notes of the scale.

Question 2) How do you convert a major scale to a major pentatonic scale?

You drop the fourth and the seventh note notes of the scale.

Question 3) How do you convert a minor scale to a minor pentatonic scale?

You drop the second and the sixth notes of the scale.

Question 4) How do you convert a major scale to a minor pentatonic scale?

You drop the second and sixth notes of the scale and flat the third and seventh notes of the scale.
