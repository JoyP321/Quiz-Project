Jazz Band Quiz Game!

This game will cover major, minor, major pentatonic, and minor pentatonic scales

Question 1) How do you convert a major scale to a minor scale?

You flat the third, sixth, and seventh notes of the scale. 1 2 3b 4 5 6b 7b 8

Question 2) How do you convert a major scale to a major pentatonic scale?

You drop the fourth and the seventh note notes of the scale. 1 2 3 _ 5 6 _ 8

Question 3) How do you convert a minor scale to a minor pentatonic scale?

You drop the second and the sixth notes of the scale. 1 _ 3 4 5 _ 7 8

Question 4) How do you convert a major scale to a minor pentatonic scale?

You drop the second and sixth notes of the scale and flat the third and seventh notes of the scale. 1 _ 3b 4 5 _ 7b 8
